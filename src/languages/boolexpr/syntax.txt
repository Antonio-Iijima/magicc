#require datatypes.list


<PROGRAM>      ::= <BOOLEXPR> | <BOOLEXPR> \n+ <PROGRAM>
<BOOLEXPR>     ::= \( <LIST> , <EXPR> \)
<ATOM>         ::= <VAR>
<EXPR>         ::= <OR>
<OR>           ::= <EXPR> \| <OR> | <AND>
<AND>          ::= <EXPR> & <AND> | <NOT>
<NOT>          ::= ~ <EXPR> | <LITERAL> | <VAR>
<LITERAL>      ::= t | f
<VAR>          ::= [a-eg-su-z]
