#require datatypes.list


<PROGRAM>  ::= <BOOLEXPR> | <BOOLEXPR> \n+ <PROGRAM>
<BOOLEXPR> ::= \( <LIST> , <EXPR> \)
<ATOM>     ::= <VAR>
<EXPR>     ::= <AND> \| <EXPR> | <AND>
<AND>      ::= <NOT> & <AND> | <NOT>
<NOT>      ::= ~ <NOT> | <LITERAL> | <VAR>
<LITERAL>  ::= t | f
<VAR>      ::= [a-eg-su-z]
